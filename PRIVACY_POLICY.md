## Introduction

Autism Typing Tutor is an educational app designed specifically for children with autism to learn typing skills through AI-powered personalized learning. We are committed to protecting the privacy of our users, especially children.

## Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

This app is designed for children under 13 years old. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without parental consent.

## Information We Collect

### Information Stored Locally on Your Device

All data is stored **locally on your device** using browser storage (localStorage). We do not transmit any personal data to external servers except for the specific AI and image services described below.

**Data collected includes:**

1. **Typing Performance Data**
   - Words typed and accuracy scores
   - Typing speed and mistakes
   - Practice session timestamps
   - Streak counts and achievements

2. **Learning Progress Data**
   - Word mastery levels (which words the child knows vs. needs practice)
   - Comprehension question results (correct/incorrect answers)
   - Skill assessment metrics
   - Level progression and XP points

3. **User Preferences**
   - App settings (font size, visual preferences)
   - Selected themes and colors
   - Virtual pet customization

4. **Gameplay Data**
   - Badge unlocks and rewards
   - Pet companion progress
   - Session history

### Why We Collect This Data

We collect performance and progress data to:
- **Personalize learning**: AI analyzes typing patterns to adapt difficulty
- **Track progress**: Show parents and children improvement over time
- **Optimize teaching**: Identify which words need more practice
- **Provide encouragement**: Unlock achievements and celebrate milestones
- **Better serve learners**: Continuously improve the educational experience

**Important**: All analysis happens **locally on your device** or through secure AI services. We do not store or access this data ourselves.

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

### OpenAI (GPT-4o-mini)
- **Purpose**: Generate educational content (words, sentences, comprehension questions)
- **Data sent**: Word difficulty preferences, content type requests
- **Data NOT sent**: Child's name, performance data, or any identifying information
- **Privacy Policy**: https://openai.com/privacy

### Unsplash
- **Purpose**: Fetch educational images for visual learning
- **Data sent**: Search keywords (e.g., "butterfly", "apple")
- **Data NOT sent**: Any personal or performance data
- **Privacy Policy**: https://unsplash.com/privacy

**Important**: These services only receive anonymous, non-identifying requests. No personal data about your child is transmitted.

## Data Storage and Security

- **All data is stored locally** on your device
- **No cloud storage**: Data never leaves your device except for AI/image requests
- **No user accounts**: No login, email, or personal information required
- **No tracking**: We do not track user behavior across apps or websites
- **No third-party analytics**: We do not use Google Analytics or similar services

## Parental Rights

As a parent or guardian, you have the right to:

1. **Review data**: View all stored data through the app's Parent Dashboard
2. **Delete data**: Clear all progress and settings at any time
3. **Control usage**: Set time limits and monitor progress
4. **Export data**: Export progress reports for educational records

### How to Delete All Data

To completely remove all stored data:
1. Go to **Settings** in the app
2. Scroll to **Privacy & Data**
3. Tap **Clear All Data**
4. Confirm deletion

Alternatively, uninstalling the app removes all stored data.

## Data Sharing

We do **NOT**:
- Sell or share data with third parties
- Use data for advertising
- Create user profiles for marketing
- Share data with social media platforms
- Transfer data to other companies
